About Dr.Mary Louis

Distinguished educator, researcher, and pioneer in data science education

Dr. L. Mary Louis is a distinguished Professor of Mathematics and Data Science at Kumaraguru College of Liberal Arts and Science, with 30+ years of academic experience. She holds a Ph.D. in Mathematics from Avinashilingam University and is an expert in stochastic modeling, data envelopment analysis, and educational technology integration, bridging mathematics with data science.

She has mentored 500+ students, supervised 3 Ph.D. scholars and numerous M.Phil. students, and received global recognition, including the Distinguished Researcher in Mathematics Award (2018) and International Innovative Researcher Award (2019).

A university rank holder throughout her academic journey, Dr. Mary Louis has published internationally, presented at global conferences, and organized several national academic events, significantly advancing mathematics and data science education.

Dr. Mary Louis has significantly advanced mathematics and data science education by leading national conferences, workshops, and seminars.
